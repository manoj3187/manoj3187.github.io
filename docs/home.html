Introduction

Centralized Animal Rescue or CAR is a technology that aims to centralize animal rescue and pet care. Targeted to help shelters and clinics that do not have the resources to host their own website or mobile apps, CAR hosts information about animal shelters, veterinary clinics, animals available for adoption and foster-care, lost/found/stray animals in the neighborhood.

      CAR helps users find their missing pets, or report stray animals. A Stray/Lost/Found report triggers alerts to be sent to many animal shelters based on proximity and availability. Animal shelter employees recieve alerts and rescue the animal or help users find their missing pets based on the type of the report.

      CAR also helps users search for animal shelters and look-up animals available for adoption and foster-care. It lets users book appointments at veterinary clinics in a centralized manner.
